Hippos: Cooler than Cats

Hippopotamus amphibius, or Hippo for short, have been around for over 50 millions years and are found mainly in sub-Saharan Africa. They are the third-largest land animal in the world.

Largest Land animals:

  1. Elephant
  2. Rhinoceros
  3. Hippos

Fun Facts:

  • Hippos can run up to 19 mph
  • Male Hippos can weigh up to 4,000 pounds
  • Hippos kill up to 2,900 humans every year
